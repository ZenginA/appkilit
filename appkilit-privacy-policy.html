Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how AppKilit ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

1. Information We Collect

AppKilit is designed with privacy in mind. The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your PIN code
  • The list of apps you choose to lock
  • Screen time settings you configure

None of this information is transmitted to our servers or any third party. It exists solely on your device and is used only to provide the core functionality of the app.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

AppKilit does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Your location
  • Your contacts or call logs
  • Keystrokes or screen content from other apps
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • The content of any app you lock

3. Permissions

AppKilit requests the following device permissions:

  • Accessibility Service: Used solely to detect which app is currently in the foreground so that the PIN lock screen can be displayed when a locked app is opened. We do not read screen content, keystrokes, or any personal data from other apps.
  • Display over other apps (SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW): Used to show the PIN lock screen on top of other applications.
  • Query all packages (QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES): Used to display the list of installed apps on your device so you can choose which ones to lock. This list never leaves your device.
  • Receive boot completed: Used to automatically restart the protection service when your device is rebooted.
  • Internet access: Used solely to load advertisements within the app.

4. Advertising

AppKilit may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising networks. These advertising partners may use cookies, device identifiers, and similar technologies to serve ads that are relevant to your interests, and to measure the performance of their ads.

Information that advertising partners may collect includes:

  • Device identifiers (such as Advertising ID)
  • Approximate location based on IP address
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)
  • App usage information for ad targeting purposes

This data is collected and processed by our advertising partners under their own privacy policies. AppKilit does not have direct access to or control over this data.

5. Data Security

All data stored by AppKilit (PIN, locked app list, settings) resides locally on your device using Android's secure DataStore. We do not operate any servers that store your personal information, and therefore there is no risk of your AppKilit data being exposed through a server-side breach.

6. Children's Privacy

AppKilit is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed as a parental control and personal privacy tool for adults and teenagers. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

7. Third-Party Services

AppKilit may contain links to or integrations with third-party services. This Privacy Policy applies only to AppKilit. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you interact with.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
